Financial Analysis

The $969K Question

DPR told the Board that closing Barcroft saves $969,542. We tried to build that number from their own data. There's a $375K gap in expenses and $121K in mystery revenue that 1,300 pages of FOIA records can't explain. DES confirmed they never validated the savings.

Partnership Revenue

The GWU Revenue They Left Out

GW's NCAA gymnastics team was paying $75K–$88K/year to practice at Barcroft, furnishing free equipment, and offering coaching support. DPR excluded all of it from the savings calculation. Rudolph flagged the omission herself. Staff dismissed it.

$33K to Maintain a Building They Want to Close

Accountability

The 2015 Promise DPR Broke

The Board directed DPR to study alternatives to County-operated gymnastics. The Phase II analysis was mandated. It was never completed. Eleven years later, DPR jumped straight to elimination without doing the work the Board ordered.
